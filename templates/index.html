Our Product.

Aawaz, is the key to empower people who are 'speech-impaired' or 'unable to speak' allowing them to communicate with other people. Our product takes advantage of the Intel Curie chip to read sensations/gestures and when combined with the application developed by us, it predicts what the user is trying to convey, acting as a means og communication.

Our Goal.

We realized that, there are many people who are unable to speak, either for a short span of time or permanantly disabled and when they try to communicate with other persons, they either have to write it or uses gestures to convey their message. But most of the time the other person is unable to understand their gestures. So we decided to accelerate their gesuters to be able to converted to voice. And then Aawaz was developed.

Our Mission.

Our application comapres the person's gestures with a predefined set of samples to find a best match. To make the prediction accurate we are continously adding samples for each gesture, at the same time also adding more gestures to widen the set of conversation messages.

Get In Touch With Us.

If you liked our idea and wanted to contribute into our project, please contact us help training our device with more and more gestures and their samples. This would help us improve the accuracy of our product, resulting in better predictions of gestures.

About Aawaz

This is a device which enables speech impaired people to convey their message in the form of voice. Individuals express their thoughts through hand movements which is then converted into speech.
